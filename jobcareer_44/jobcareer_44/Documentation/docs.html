Post a new Job

JobCareer Theme comes with the marvelous feature where employers (recruiters) can post jobs . Follow following steps to post a new job on JobCareer site: 

Step 1- Add Job Details

To post a new job create an employer account and fill all required fields and description. Follow steps to configure fields as:

  1. Enter Job Title, description, Email Address and Username
  2. Select Job Type (Freelance, Full time, internship, part time, temporary, volunteer)
  3. Select Specialism (Accountancy, Banking etc.)
  4. Select Offered Salary Range (Admin will setup salary range and employer can select as per his requirements.)
  5. Determine Career Level (Manager, Officer, Executive etc.)
  6. Select Experience, Gender, Industry, Qualification as per job specifications.
  7. Enter Location (Country, City, Complete Address) for job's location
  8. Find on Map (Latitude, Longitude) field will help you search location on Map 

Click Next Button to proceed to next step to post job.

Step 2 â Select Packages & Payments

  1. Select Job Package i-e Basic, Standard, Gold etc. to initiate payment procedure to post your job.
    Here, all job packages will be listed and employer can buy a package from here to post a job and from that specific purchased packages number of allowed job posting will be consumed.
  2. Make this Job Featured: Select the option if you want your job to appear featured on your site. (It depends on featured settings configured by admin to show featured jobs)
  3. Choose Payment gateway as you desire, By default theme have (Paypal, Bank Transfer, Authorize.net and Skrill)  

Select the package and payment gateway, than it will direct you to the payment page  according to the payment gateway selected. 

Step 3 â Confirmation

After successful payment to the subscribed package for job it will redirect to confirmation page. 

On Confirmation page all payment information regarding subscribed package will be shown.

  1. Total charges = Package Price (+ Featured price (if selected)
  2. Grand Total = Total Charges + VAT

Notification "Created Successfully" will appear after job has been successfully posted on your site. All Done!
